How SPAN works.

01
Algorithmic Screening
Every stock is evaluated against 6 quantitative criteria: revenue growth, margin expansion, debt levels, free cash flow yield, earnings quality, and momentum.
02
DCF Valuation
A discounted cash flow model projects 10-year free cash flow using analyst consensus estimates, discounted at WACC to determine intrinsic value and upside.
03
SPAN Score
Screening checks (40%) + DCF margin of safety (40%) + 3-year backtest performance (20%) are combined into a single 0–100 SPAN Score.
04
Signal Classification
Scores ≥70 classify as Buy, 45–69 as Hold, and below 45 as Sell. Signals update each market day as new earnings and filings are processed.
