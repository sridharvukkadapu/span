How SPAN Scores Work ▾
01 / CHECKS
Multi-Factor Checks
Each ticker is evaluated against 6 fundamental and technical checks: revenue growth, margin expansion, earnings beat, institutional flow, RSI zone, and trend strength.
02 / SCORE
Composite Score
Checks are weighted and combined into a 0–100 score. BUY ≥ 70, HOLD 45–69, SELL < 45. Scores refresh every 15 minutes during market hours.
03 / SIGNAL
Signal Generation
Signal transitions require two consecutive periods above/below the threshold to reduce noise. Signals are not financial advice — always do your own research.
04 / TREND
Price Momentum
The Trend column shows 30-day price momentum as a percentage, colour-coded green (positive), red (negative). Used as a secondary signal confirmation factor.
